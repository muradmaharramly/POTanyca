About Us

Welcome to POTanyca, nestled in the heart of Berndorf, Germany. We are a beloved destination for flower enthusiasts and those seeking a touch of nature's beauty. Our mission is to provide an exceptional experience to our guests, offering a wide array of stunning floral arrangements in a warm, inviting, and tranquil setting. We strive to infuse a sense of serenity into the city with our charming space, where you can explore our exquisite flowers and enjoy the soothing ambiance. Come and relax amidst our beautifully arranged blooms and let the natural beauty uplift your day.
